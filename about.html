About Me

This is some information about me!

Hello! I'm Nathan, a passionate software developer with a love for creating innovative solutions. In my free time, I enjoy exploring new technologies, contributing to open-source projects, and enhancing my skills through continuous learning.

I've worked on several projects ranging from small personal websites to larger applications. My goal is to build user-friendly and efficient software that meets the needs of users. I'm always excited to take on new challenges and collaborate with others in the tech community.

I'm currently studying Full Stack Software Development at Zindua school while simultaneously preparing for C1 German examinations. I have been interested in code and technologies since I was a child about 12 years old and at the moment I can work well with HTML and Bootstrap and I am working on learning better CSS skills, Tailwind, JavaScript, and React.

Check out my portfolio to see some of the projects I've worked on, and feel free to reach out if you'd like to connect or collaborate!
